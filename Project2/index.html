A Gaming Memory

Sly Cooper 2: Band of Thieves

Game Title: Sly Cooper 2: Band of Thieves

Platform: PS2

When: 2004

The Memory

I think this was one of the very first games I ever played when I really started becoming conscious as a human being. You know, instead of being completely oblivious to the world like most 4 year olds. It's one of my earliest gaming memories, sitting in front of the TV with my older brother trying to steal the mechanical body parts of the games big bad, "Clockwerk."

I remember being absolutely fascinated by this game. It was probably what really set me on a path of generally preferring single player games over multiplayer because the story in this game was just so fun, cheesy, and easy to follow when I first played it. I played it again just a couple months back and the games are still just as fantastic as they were in my childhood.
