A Memory That Stuck

Some media hits you once and quietly rewires your taste.

Midnight Drives + Music Videos

  • Medium: Music + image
  • When: Late teens
  • Why it matters: It taught me mood is a tool

I remember sitting in a parked car with the engine still ticking, watching a music video on a phone screen, and realizing how much emotion you can build with a few decisions: where the light hits, how the bass lands, when the cut happens, when it doesn’t. That tiny moment made me care about pacing, texture, and atmosphere — not just “what happens,” but how it feels while it happens.

That’s the kind of thinking I carry into sound design, photography, and film. I chase the moment where the audience goes quiet because the scene suddenly feels real.
